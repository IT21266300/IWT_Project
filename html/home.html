Local Trust Bank

Local Trust Bank was founded in 1999 to introduce the new level of financial services worldwide. We are still dedicated to the success of our clients, both individual and corporate. At Gain Bank, we are guided by a common purpose to help make financial lives better by connecting clients and communities to the resources they need to be successful. We are driving growth – helping to create jobs, develop communities, foster economic mobility and address society’s biggest challenges – while managing risk and providing a return to our clients and our shareholders
